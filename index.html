About

Dean Ver Tinio is a video editor and content strategist with a proven track record of delivering impactful content for top-tier YouTube creators and leading esports organizations. He has worked with some of the biggest names in the digital content space, including ZHC, Chad Wild Clay, Cody Ko, and Matthew Beem, along with premier esports teams like TSM and FlyQuest.

Dean's expertise lies in transforming raw footage into compelling narratives that keep audiences engaged. He has managed large-scale productions for multi-million subscriber channels, crafted retention-focused shortform content, and executed quick-turnaround comedy edits with precision and style.

From sponsor-backed challenge videos to high-energy gaming content, Dean's ability to balance storytelling with dynamic pacing has made him a go-to editor for creators and brands looking to elevate their content.
